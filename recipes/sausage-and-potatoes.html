Sausage and Potaotes and Peppers

Decription

This savory recipe is always a comfy treat for those cold winter days. Perfectly filling and quick to make, add this to your book for any intimate occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds of smokes sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
