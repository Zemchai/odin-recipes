Grandma's Mac N' Cheese

Description

This yellow goodness will take you right back to childhood. Nothings says comfort more than this timeless meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 Â½ cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup Colby & Monterey Jack, shredded
  • 6-8 oz Velveeta cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar, shredded
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup smoked cheddar cheese, shredded
  • paprika (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Cook macaroni until just al dente or a little under al dente.
  3. Drain pasta and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, add milk, heavy cream & cheeses (except for the smoked cheddar).
  5. Stir to combine.
  6. Taste the milk mixture and add salt & pepper until it has a good taste. (Take liberty here!)
  7. When you are content with the taste, add the eggs. Stir until well combined.
  8. Add macaroni to the baking dish.
  9. Pour cheese mixture over macaroni. Make sure cheese is well distributed.
  10. Top with the smoked cheddar cheese. Sprinkle with paprika and/or black pepper, if desired.
  11. Bake for 35-45 minutes. Do not overbake. It may be a bit jiggly when you take it out of the oven. It will firm up as it cool.
