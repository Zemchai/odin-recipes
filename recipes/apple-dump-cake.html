Collegiate Apple Dump Cake

Description

Perfect for those tight budget times. All you need is a crockpot and a few simple ingredients even a college student could have. This is the perfect replacement for apple pie!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans Apple Pie Filling
  • 1 box Yellow Cake Mix
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ cup pecans, chopped; optional

Steps

  1. In a 5-6 quart crockpot dump the 2 cans of apple pie filling.
  2. In a bowl mix together the cake mix, cinnamon, pecans, and melted butter. Crumble the cake mix over the apple pie filling.
  3. Cover and cook on high for 2 hours.
  4. Serve warm with ice cream
